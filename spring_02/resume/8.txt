-SEL: a prominent approach within the machine learning paradigm that combines the predictions of base-level models and improves the overall performance of predictions in order to reduce the errors and biases of each model.
-objectives is to develop an enhanced BT detection and segmentation framework based on densely connected CNN with a stack ensemble approach
-the strengths of each base model are leveraged in order to capture different aspects of the BT MRI for better segmentation
-the architecture:
upload datasets
data preprocessing: shuffle, resize, flip (horizontai and vertical)
data splitting (70-10-20)
level-0 (weak learners): 3D CNN, AlexNet, ResNet50, VGG-16, VGG19, MobileNet-V3
prediction of level-0 models
level-1 (meta layer): DenseNet201)
output

-datasets: 5 public datasets including large Kaggle repositories (Datasets I and II) and the prestigious BraTS Challenge series (2020, 2021, 2023), all images resized to 512x512
